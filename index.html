About
AUP

ALARD University Pune, a legacy that spans a generation, is more than just a university. It is where dreams become reality, pupils become pioneers, and success stands at the precipice of hard work. Our acronym ALARD stands for Attitude, Leadership, Alertness, Readiness, and Dedication. Founded in 1999 under the visionary leadership of Dr. L. R. Yadav, the mission was to sow seeds of knowledge and skill in students' minds, fostering entrepreneurship to enrich the lives and careers of youth, thus becoming a catalyst for change in both individual lives and society.

ALARD University Pune is now synonymous with quality education in the country, which curates and nurtures future professionals to lead the nation. It has emerged as one of Pune's most esteemed universities and is now looking to reach the world with an advanced and efficient learning model.
